# Research Report on Generative AI Applications

## 1. Healthcare
**Description:**  
Generative AI is used to create synthetic medical data, generate diagnostic reports, and assist in drug discovery

**Business Value:**  
Reduces the cost of clinical  trials and speeds up diagnosis

**Technical Challenges:**  
Data privacy, need for large high-quality datasets, and model interpretability

**Weak Points:**  
Potential bias in generated data and risk of incorrect medical advice.

## 2. Education
**Description:**  
AI tutors generate personalized learning materials and quizzes for students based on performance

**Business Value:**  
Automates content creation, improves engagement, reduces teacher workload.

**Technical Challenges:**  
Content accuracy, language adaptation, and plagiarism detection.

**Weak Points:**  
Dependence on data quality and lack of emotional intelligence.

**Existing Implementations:**  
- Khanmigo (Khan Academy) - ChatGPT-powered tutor.  
- Duolingo Max - Uses GPT-4 to explain and simulate dialogues.
- ailearna.com - Learn english with AI


## 3. Software Development
